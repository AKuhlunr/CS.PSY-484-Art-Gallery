Group 4 Final Project: VR Art

Welcome to VR Art! A whole new way to experience Art. This is an interactive art experience where players can transport themselves to exciting new galleries from the comfort of their own homes

Instructions:
- You can move by using the left controller thumbstick
- Use the right controller thumbstick to turn your camera
- In order to interact with the paintings, use your hand to press the big red button beneath each painting. This will play a brief description about the painting.

Enjoy!
